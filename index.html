Business Analyst

Pristina, Kosovo Full Time

Job Description


Our innovative consultancy is seeking a highly skilled Business Analyst that will join us for an interesting and challenging role. This role will require communication and interaction with external clients, and our internal analytics team to ensure the client’s requirements are well understood, and the appropriate solution is designed and delivered. Apart from gathering and analyzing client’s requirements, in this role, you will be responsible for analyzing and interpreting data related to our client’s business needs, identifying trends and insights and providing recommendations to optimize performance.



Responsibility

To be successful in this role, you should have a strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as experience with data analysis tools. You should also be a self-starter with the ability to work independently and in a team environment.


  • Identifying and defining the business needs of the clients and stakeholders, assisting with resolving business problems and designing methodologies to meet the needs of the business

  • Combining business analytics, data mining, and data visualization, to help organizations to make more data-driven decisions

  • Analyzing and interpreting data from multiple sources, including web analytics, customer behavior, and sales data

  • Analyze, optimize, and built business models for our clients including but not limited to: products, pricing, promotions, competitor trends, activities, KPIs, etc.

  • Proactively create custom and in-depth analysis and research focused to show value, insight, and missed opportunities

  • Define metrics to evaluate performance and build reporting/dashboards to track performance and maintain it.

  • Developing and maintaining dashboards and reports to track key performance indicators and provide insights to stakeholders

  • Communicating with business owners to define business requirements, and ensure that their needs are met with the agreed standard and quality

  • Creating and communicating insight and recommendations that help our clients to influence, support and execute strategic business decisions.


Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree (Master’s degree is desirable)

  • 2+ years of experience in the Data Analytics field

  • Fluent in English.

  • Strong analytical, data mining, modeling, and problem-solving skills.

  • Drive execution, initiate projects, and manage them from start to end.

  • Process-oriented, well-organized, autonomous learner.

  • Experience working with Excel and MS office.

  • Experience working with Tableau, Power Bi, or other BI tools.

  • Experience in data modeling using SPSS, Python, or R.

  • Excellent written and oral communication and interpersonal skills
